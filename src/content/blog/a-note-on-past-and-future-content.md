I've used AI to generate the writing for posts on this blog. As much as I prefer to think of it as an "Editor that also fleshes out ideas", a lot of voice is lost in the process. I don't plan on stopping to use AI to generate content, or in my daily work, probably ever. I generated the source code for this post through OpenClaw. I will, however, be making a permanent change to divide all content into "Posts" - stuff you can read that was typed out with my own ten fingers (like this), and "AI Posts" - things that were interesting to share after a coding session, tutorials, guides, and similar content to what I've written so far. This is stuff that wouldn't make it to the internet otherwise, and it has value to me - so maybe you'll like it too!

I use sublime text - what's your manual text editor of choice?
