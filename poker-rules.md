# Poker Game Rules

## General Information

**Buy-in:** $20

**Chip Denominations:**
- 10¢
- 25¢
- $1
- No other denominations

## Game-Specific Betting Rules

| Game Type | Betting Structure |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Texas Hold'em** | Big blind and small blind. No ante. |
| **Omaha** | Big blind and small blind. No ante. |
| **Seven Card Stud** | Ante required. Bring-in bet (lowest upcard must bring it in). |
| **Short Deck** | Ante instead of blinds. No small blind or big blind. |
